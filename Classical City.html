CLASSICAL CITY

The Classical City is a fresh take on the grand cities and structures of Europe, combining architectural elements of locations such as Budapest, Pisa, Prague, and Rome

BUILDINGS

The Classical City features hundreds of buildings each with its own unique design. Here you can find the highlights




TOWN HALL

The town hall is located near the heart of the city and serves as both its main political structure and as a symbol of the city.

Constructed almost entirely of stone, the town hall features a striking design inside and out. On the inside it features a rich hardwood floor with wool carpet as well as chiseled quartz details




CASTLE

The Castle, centrally located, serves as both the city's main defensive structure and as the home of the royal family. It features four main towers, one of which is taller to allow for increased vision.

Inside the walls are stables, an armory, and animal enclosures as well as vegetable farms that allow the Castle to hold out even during a seige. It even includes a chapel should traveling to the Cathedral be an inconvenience

The interior includes the throne, dining table, and lounging area. Access to the four towers is available in the corners of structure. Situated above the lounging area is the king's royal living quarters




CATHEDRAL

The Cathedral serves as the main place of worship for the city. Located adjacent to the central square, it features an imposing and majestic silhouette.
