ABOUT

Minecraft Cities is a site created to display the cities that I have painstakingly created over the past several years. So far three cities have been built, the first of these being the Southern European inspired "Classical City" soon followed by the Southeast Asian themed "Oriental City" and lastly the "Desert City" inspired by Constantinople and Middle Eastern influences.
